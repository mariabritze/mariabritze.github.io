How do you write in my voice if you don't know me?
+

We start with a deep onboarding call — your story, values, how you talk about your product. The first month is calibration. By month two, most clients say it sounds exactly like them.

Inbound inquiries typically appear in weeks 6–10 on LinkedIn. Email revenue impact is faster — especially fair follow-up sequences. This is a compounding system, not an instant fix.

Retainers run on 3-month minimum commitments. The once-off automation build is a single project with no ongoing commitment.
